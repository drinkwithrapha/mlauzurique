1800 Tysons First Floor Amenity Space

Location Tysons, VA Client CBRE Investment Management Status Completed 2019 Services Interior Architecture Program Class A Office · Lobby renovation · Common areas

1800 Tysons Boulevard is a premier Class A office tower serving the Northern Virginia business community. The interior renovation transformed the building's common areas to meet the expectations of today's most discerning corporate tenants.

The redesigned lobby creates a striking first impression with contemporary finishes, strategic lighting, and flexible seating areas that support both brief meetings and focused work. The design reinforces the building's position as a premier business address in the Tysons corridor.

Amenity Spaces

The tenant amenity program creates opportunities for collaboration, wellness, and work beyond the traditional office suite. These shared spaces enhance the building's value proposition while fostering a sense of community.
