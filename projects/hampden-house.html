Hampden House

Location Bethesda, MD Client JBG Smith Status Completed 2020 Services Interior Architecture Program 12-story · 360 units · Luxury amenities

Hampden House represents the pinnacle of luxury residential living in Bethesda's most sought-after neighborhood. The interior design creates a seamless connection between the building's elegant architecture and the refined lifestyle expectations of its discerning residents.

The design philosophy centers on understated luxury—clean lines, premium materials, and thoughtfully curated details that speak to quality without ostentation. Natural light floods the common spaces, accentuating the warmth of wood tones and the sophistication of stone finishes.
