The Witmer

Location Arlington, VA Client Kimco Realty Corporation Status Completed 2019 Services Architecture / Planning / Interiors Program 25-story · 446,400 sf · 440 units · 11,400 sf retail

Pentagon Centre is envisioned to be a transit-oriented, high-density, mixed-use community with more than 1.8 million-sf of redevelopment. The Witmer, the first building of this redevelopment, is a 440-unit luxury apartment tower with an array of amenities.

Designed to be the tallest building in Pentagon City at 270 feet high, the tower consists of 18 residential floors over a six-story parking garage. The contemporary building aesthetic contrasts from the horizon of masonry buildings with soaring glass panels layered with dramatic, geometrical precast pillars.

Design

The main facades of the residential floors showcase soaring glass panels layered with dramatic, geometrical precast pillars. Recessed balconies extend to the top of the tower. Large storefront window systems maximize natural light for the lobby and reception areas.

Pleated metal screen walls enclose the multi-story parking garage, transforming it into an art wall illuminated with LED lighting features at night. The top of the building is articulated with dramatic precast concrete columns that frame the roof terrace with 360-degree views.
