Manuel Lauzurique-Abiega

Interior Architect

Manuel's portfolio spans luxury multifamily residences and commercial office environments, from amenity spaces and lobbies to full-building interior programs. With more than 28 years of experience, he thrives at finding ways to inject authenticity and character into every space — transforming functional requirements into environments that tell a story.

From concept development through construction administration, his acute command of FF&E specification and procurement ensures every detail is intentional. A strategic advocate for design excellence, Manuel has built lasting client relationships and directed high-performing creative teams throughout his career at WDG Interior Architecture in Washington, DC.
